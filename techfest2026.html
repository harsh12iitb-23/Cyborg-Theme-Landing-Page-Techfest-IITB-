Theme 2026

Cyborg Evolution:
Beyond Human Limits

The 2026 theme confronts the defining question of our era: as machines grow more human and humans grow more machine, where does one end and the other begin? Techfest 2026 is an invitation to explore that vanishing line.

From brain-computer interfaces and neural enhancement to synthetic biology and exoskeletal engineering, our events span the full spectrum of human augmentation — celebrating both the breakthroughs and the ethical conversations they demand.

This is not science fiction. It is the science of now.

Signature Events

What awaits you

EVT — 001
RoboWars

250-kg combat robots clash in an arena where engineering precision meets raw destruction. Asia's most-watched bot battle, now with a cyborg augmentation twist.

EVT — 002
BrainHack

48-hour hackathon focused on brain-computer interface prototypes. Build real-time EEG applications, neural decoders, or cognitive enhancement tools.

EVT — 003
NeuralCraft

AI model-building competition where participants design architectures that mimic human cognitive processes — memory, reasoning, and intuition.

EVT — 004
Exo-Sprint

Design and race a wearable exoskeletal aid. Teams compete on speed, endurance enhancement, and load-carrying capacity with their self-built prototypes.

EVT — 005
CipherGrid

A multi-stage cryptography and cybersecurity challenge. Navigate encrypted labyrinths, intercept simulated signals, and crack machine-generated ciphers.

EVT — 006
GenomeForge

Synthetic biology design challenge. Teams propose and model CRISPR-based solutions to real healthcare and environmental problems using provided bioinformatics toolkits.
